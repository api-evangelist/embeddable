When you follow interesting accounts on Twitter, it’s easy to stay connected with what’s most meaningful to you.Today we’re excited to launch the Follow Button, a new way to discover and instantly follow Twitter accounts directly from the websites you visit every day.More than 50 sites have added the Follow Button today, making it easy for you to discover the Twitter accounts of your favorite reporters, athletes, celebrities, and other personalities.Using the Follow Button is as simple as a single click.You can also see the profile and latest Tweets of the account you want to follow by clicking the username next to the Button.
